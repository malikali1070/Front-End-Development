One UI or IOS ?


• NEW FEATURES


The years, a general trend has been seen that Samsung is the first one to bring forth new and amazing software features in their One UI operating system and these features are delayed in Apple’s iOS for one to two years. However,when they do best eventually come to iOS, they are slightly enhanced and they feel more polished.But, the question is, are they worth the wait?The decision is yours!





• CUSTOMIZATION



One of the major strengths of android user interfaces is that they offer tons of customization, and Samsung’s One UI is no different. You can change the shape of icons, the color of menus, install custom icon packs according to your liking or even switch to an entirely different system launcher that’s the beauty of android. On the other hand, unfortunately, Apple does not give you this freedom and restricts most of the customisation. If you are a fan of customization and want to tweak your phone the way you like it then go for Samsung!





• SOFTWARE UPDATES



Regular software updates are a must for an engaging and enjoyable smartphone experience and while both Samsung and Apple are really good at providing software updates, Apple has an upper hand in this department. Samsung promises 3 years of major android updates and 5 years of security patch updates for its flagship devices, whereas Apple provides 6 years of major OS updates. So, if you plan to use your smartphone for a really long time period, definitely Apple would be the better choice. However, if you upgrade smartphones every 2 to 3 years, like most people do, this wouldn't make any difference and you can go with any of the two
