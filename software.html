HARDWARE


• CAMERAS


Most people may say : “ iPhones have the best cameras.”, while this statement may be true a couple years back, the present situation is different. The latest Samsung flagship S21 Ultra has a better camera system than iPhone 13 pro max according to many tech reviewers. This also reflects the improvements Samsung has brought to its camera hardware and photo capturing algorithms. Moreover, with the use of telephoto camera, the maximum zoom capabilities of Samsung are much greater than Apple. Samsung also uses a much higher megapixel count for their cameras which results in photos with plenty of sharpness. But Apple has its strengths too, the videos captured by iPhone cameras are still unmatchable in quality. So, in conclusion, both Samsung and Apple have a great camera system, both have large camera sensors, optical image stabilisation and excellent image processing.It's a tie!

• PROCESSOR



Samsung uses Qualcomm Snapdragon or their own Exynos processors and Apple uses their A-series bionic chipsets. While all these chipsets are extremely fast and it's really hard to spot any difference between their performances, when you dive deep and look closely, the A-series bionic chipsets are more capable with better optimizations, then comes Qualcomm Snapdragon and then EXYNOS.
